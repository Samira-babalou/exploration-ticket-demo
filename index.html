What is the Exploration Ticket?

The Exploration Ticket supports students who want to investigate an innovative idea before moving into prototyping or startup development. It provides financial support and creative freedom to test assumptions, explore opportunities, build networks, and strengthen innovation skills.

Explore Opportunities

Understand users, markets, and societal needs.

Build Knowledge

Research technologies, trends, and innovation potential.

Prepare the Next Step

Generate evidence for future prototyping and transfer funding.
